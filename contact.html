FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and approach.

What does AI-powered digital marketing mean?

AI-powered digital marketing means we leverage artificial intelligence tools and algorithms to enhance traditional marketing strategies. This includes using AI for data analysis, content creation assistance, automation of repetitive tasks, personalized customer experiences, and predictive analytics to make more informed marketing decisions.

How long does it typically take to see results?

While some improvements can be seen immediately (such as workflow efficiencies from automation), most digital marketing efforts take time to show measurable results. Typically, clients begin seeing noticeable improvements within 60-90 days. SEO strategies might take 3-6 months for significant ranking improvements, while email marketing and paid advertising strategies can show results more quickly.

Do you offer ongoing support after implementation?

Yes, we offer various ongoing support packages tailored to your needs. These can include regular strategy sessions, technical support, analytics reporting, content updates, and continuous optimization of your digital assets. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients to ensure sustained growth.

What makes your SEO approach different?

Our SEO FlashLight tool uses AI to identify high-impact opportunities that traditional SEO audits might miss. We focus on actionable recommendations rather than overwhelming you with data. Additionally, we prioritize sustainable, white-hat SEO practices that align with your overall business goals rather than chasing algorithm changes.

How do you price your services?

We offer flexible pricing models depending on your needs. This includes project-based pricing for one-time initiatives, monthly retainers for ongoing services, and custom packages that combine various services. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees and work to create solutions that fit within your budget constraints.

What industries do you specialize in?

While we work with businesses across many sectors, we have particular expertise in professional services, nonprofits, digital publishing, and wellness industries. Our experience in these areas allows us to understand industry-specific challenges and opportunities, but our AI-powered approach can be adapted to virtually any business model.
