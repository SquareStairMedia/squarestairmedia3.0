Our Story

Bridging Technology and Human Creativity

Founded in 2023 by Julie C. in Toronto, SquareStair Media was born from a passion for making cutting-edge AI and digital strategy tools accessible to small businesses and nonprofits.

Frustrated by cookie-cutter solutions and a lack of AI tools being offered to business owners, Julie built SquareStair Media to offer lean, smart, customizable systems powered by modern technology and a personal touch.
